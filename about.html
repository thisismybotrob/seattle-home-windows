Our Story

Why We Do What We Do

Seattle Home Windows was founded by Sergei Fedorov with a straightforward belief: homeowners shouldn't have to pay thousands for a full window replacement when only the glass has failed.

After years in the construction and home improvement industry, Sergei kept seeing the same problem. Window companies would come out, look at a foggy window, and quote $800–$1,200 per window for a full-frame replacement. The truth? In most cases, only the insulated glass unit (IGU) needs replacing — at a fraction of the cost.

That gap between what homeowners needed and what they were being sold became the foundation of Seattle Home Windows. We specialize in glass-only replacement: removing the failed unit from your existing frame and installing a brand-new, factory-sealed IGU. Same window. Same frame. Clear view. 60-70% savings.

What started as one man with a van has grown into a trusted local business, serving hundreds of Seattle homeowners and dozens of property managers. But the mission hasn't changed: do honest work at fair prices, and let the results speak for themselves.
